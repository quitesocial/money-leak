ML-92 [DEV] Home enhancements handoff
Date: 2026-06-17
Project: Money Leak
Version after task: 1.27.1

Summary

ML-92 is implemented and manually tested. Home was updated to match Figma node
272:4270 more closely: the balance block is left-aligned with a Balance
subheader, Add/Spend buttons were tightened, Today Summary was removed, and the
Home feed heading is now Transactions.

Manual QA status

User completed manual testing after the final visual spacing tweaks and reported
everything OK.

Key product behavior implemented

- Home title remains at the top.
- Balance section now has a localized Balance subheader.
- Current balance remains calculated from existing business logic:
  active balance entries minus active/non-deleted expense transactions.
- Balance amount is left-aligned and still uses formatMoneyAmount with the
  selected Settings currency.
- Add and Spend remain equal-width pill buttons in one row.
- Add routes to /add-balance.
- Spend routes to /add-transaction.
- Buttons use expo-symbols SymbolView with arrow.down.left and arrow.up.right.
- Today Summary no longer renders on Home.
- Home no longer calls calculateDailyReviewSummary just for UI.
- Visible Home feed heading was renamed from History to Transactions.
- Transactions heading sits below the Home period segmented control, matching
  the final visual review.
- More remains in the Transactions header and routes to Analytics & Leaks.
- Home period selector remains Today, Yesterday, and This week only.
- Choose date is not exposed on Home.
- Home feed remains the existing newest-first union feed of expense transactions
  and balance additions.
- Feed filtering and sorting semantics were preserved.
- Expense transaction swipe edit/delete behavior was preserved.
- Balance row swipe edit/delete behavior was preserved as it existed before this
  task.
- Bottom tabs remain Home, Analytics & Leaks, and Settings.

Important visual tweaks made during review

- Rechecked Figma node 272:4270 and the user-provided side-by-side screenshots.
- Changed Home section typography to better match Figma:
  New York headings use iOS weight 500 instead of 700.
- Segment control typography was adjusted to 15/20 with regular 400 labels and
  selected 600 labels.
- Transaction row category and amount weights were reduced from 700 to 600.
- More text weight was reduced to 400.
- Transactions heading was moved below the period selector.
- Gap between Transactions heading and the transaction list was reduced by
  grouping the Transactions header and list in transactionsGroup with gap 8.
- Transaction cards now use justifyContent: flex-start so rows with only two
  text lines do not look vertically pushed down inside the 100px row.
